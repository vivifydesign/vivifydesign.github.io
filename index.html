DAVIDsTEA Mobile Application

This design was not commisioned and is not affiliated with DAVIDsTEA or its partners

This is a concept design for a DAVIDsTEA mobile application we designed. We use the popular DAVIDsTEA teal color as a primary color in the design. The application would allow users to browse new items and promotions at their nearby store as well as create a wishlist to share with their friends. Users could also track their rewards by connecting their account with their device.

Workability Website VCH

The Workability website was created to help staff find information ahout returning to worse after a leave of absence. For this design, we went with a bright color palette to create relationships between the problems and the color. In this image, "Returning To Work" is green throughout the site, so that users will make the connection and navigate their way easier through the site.

Medical Staff Profile Display

The Display Profiles web application was designed and developed for an Emergency Department team website at Vancouver Coastal Health. It displays staff name, department, title, and contact information. Staff use the web app to get to know their colleagues and reduce the risk of incorrect referrals. Users can search by name and title.

  • Date: July 2016
  • Service: Web Development
